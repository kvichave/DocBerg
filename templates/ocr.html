How to listen to your PDF/Image document

Listening to your PDF/Image documents in your desired language is simple with DocBergFM. Here's how to get started:

1. Select Doc/Image

Select the PDF/Image document you want to listen to. Our webapplication allows you to easily upload and select the PDF document you want to listen to.

Next

2. Select Language

Once the PDF or Image is loaded, select the language you want to listen to. Our intuitive interface allows you to quickly and easily navigate to any language you want.

Next

3. Listen/Download

Click the play button to start listening. Our advanced text-to-speech technology will read your document/Image out loud in your selected Language, allowing you to sit back, relax, and you can also download it & share with others also.
